Our Specialities

Masala Tea

Masala chai is a flavoured tea beverage made by brewing black tea with a mixture of aromatic Indian spices and herbs.

Black Tea

Black tea is more oxidized than oolong, green, and white teas. Black tea is stronger in flavour than the less oxidized teas.

Green Tea

Green tea is made from Camellia sinensis leaves that donot undergo the oxidation process used to make oolong teas and black teas.
